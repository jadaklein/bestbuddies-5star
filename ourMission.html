What is a 5-Star Representative?

The 5-Star Team is a Best Buddies volunteer-driven team dedicated to the education, engagement, and empowerment of leaders through peer-peer support and innovation.

Here you will find more description on what it means to be part of the 5-Star Team.

GENERAL INFO

How does it work?

The Best Buddies 5-Star Team is comprised of student leaders who live out the mission of inclusion and friendship. These leaders will have the honor to influence the HOW and WHY behind the operation of the Friendship Programs.

The 5-Star Team meets bi-monthly with state staff to provide feedback, inform trainings, create resources, and help lead the charge in furthering the Best Buddies mission.


As a 5-Star Representative, you will be assigned a color group comprised of the Best Buddies chapters in your immediate area (this could be anywhere from 5-15 schools) and you'll act as their representative.

RESPONSIBILITIES

What is the Commitment? (Expectations)

Participation: The 5-Star Team is a one year requirement (eligible for reelection)

Bi-Monthly Meetings: 5-Star reps will have conference calls or in-person meetings with other 5-Star members

Co-lead Trainings: These include but are not limited to conference calls within your community

Be Present and Participate: in Friendship Walk Kick Offs and Local Leadership Training Days

Create New, Relevant, and Innovative Content: videos, resources, trainings, etc. to share with peers

PURPOSE (GROWTH & LEADERSHIP)

The "Why"

Provide Feedback: You can share your thoughts to the Best Buddies Staff and influence future trainings

Community Support: You create helpful materials, resources, and discussions for chapters in your community

Mediation: You serve as an important means of communication between chapters in your community and the Best Buddies Staff to help make improvements and share ideas
